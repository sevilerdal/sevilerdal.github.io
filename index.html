About Me

I'm a 26-year-old developer living in Eskişehir, Turkey. I am self-employed and have created mobile games for clients or for publishing through Umay Game Studio, which I co-founded.

I started developing games and apps as a hobby 6 years ago. When I decided to pursue development as a career, I began working full-time in the field. I love a good problem to challenge me.

I developed games for mainly Android and Windows. I specialize in developing mobile hyper/hybrid casual games and have been actively involved in every step of game publishing, from design to publishing and advertising.

I am currently seeking a position in Unity Game Development. If you are interested, please check out my projects and GitHub!
